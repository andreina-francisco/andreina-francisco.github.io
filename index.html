I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Pharmaceutical Bioinformatics Research Group led by Professor Ola Spjuth at the Department of Pharmaceutical Biosciences, Uppsala University.

I also teach the Artificial Intelligence course at the Department of Information Technology, Uppsala University.

I was a Research Fellow working in the group of Professor Roland H. C. Yap at the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore.

My doctoral research was carried out at the Department of Information Technology, Uppsala University, where I was a member of the Optimisation Group.

In my free time I enjoy chasing my four year-old, hanging out with friends, reading detective novels, knitting, eating out, travelling, and experiencing new things.
