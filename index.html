María Andreína Francisco Rodríguez

"Who in the world am I? Ah, that's the great puzzle."
— Lewis Carroll, Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the group of Associate Professor Ola Spjuth at the Department of Pharmaceutical Biosciences, Uppsala University.

I was a lecturer at the Department of Information Technology, Uppsala University, where I taught Program Design and Data Structures (spring 2020), Imperative & Object-Oriented Programming Methodology (autumn 2019), and Semantics of Programming Languages (autumn 2019).

I was a research fellow working in the group of Professor Roland H. C. Yap at the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore.

My doctoral research was carried out at the Department of Information Technology, Uppsala University, where I was a member of the Optimisation Group.
